Dosa:The taste of South India


A dosa, dosai or dosay is a thin, savoury crepe in Indian cuisine

Two dosa rest next to a dollop of butter on a plantain leaf: The sauces have separate bowks. Achaya, references in the Sangam literature suggest that dosa was already in use in the ancient Tamil country around the first century CE. However, according to historian P. Thankappan Nair, dosa originated in the town of Udupi in Karnataka.
